github talk to dev team
  • Why did you start AEPoS?
    • We receive a lot of requests for Ethereum based networks with customizations on the consensus protocol, throughput and gas modifications and wallet customizations. Back in October 2017 together with the community of Auxilium, who are the altruistic decentralized network, we decided to migrate the network from old Bitcoin based implementation with Proof of Stake and coin-age idea borrowed from Peercoin. With further development of AEPoS we are solving the problems that many industry projects: infrastructure complexity, wallets customization, transaction fees customizations, transactions throughput and many others. AEPoS provides enterprises, startups and communities with the solution and level of support that makes deployment of proprietary networks pain-free and smooth.
